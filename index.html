I start working when I was 12 years old, 1978, I did all kinds of work, from 1988 to 1994 I work at Lisbon Dance Company (Companhia de Dança de Lisboa) as a light Technician/Repair all our material & Create Props to use on stage, from all the jobs I did this this was the one I love the most. From 1995-97 work as Volunteer to help street kids like me who live or pass all day outside because their parents where working all day. 1997-1999 got back to the Dance Company,I work almost for free because it was in serious trouble, no money, I went to live there (an old palace that over the years I help to restore with the aid of Lisbon Council).In 2000 I came to England looking for a better life, I work at night at New Convent Garden Market, Vauxhall, loading delivery Vans with fruit & vegetables from August to November 2000 & that's when I got very ill and thanks to the NHS who save my life and gave me a house (2005) to live & money to eat, only now, 2017, I'm almost at the end of this unspoken journey. In this moment, November 2017, I'm finishing a liver treatment at King's College, ends firsts days December 2017 (I had this desease for 20 years), I use a walking stick to walk (in 1 year time I'll have an operation to my foot in order to be able to walk again), I like to help other people and contribute for us to have a better society.

So, since 2005 till today,November 2017 I had to learn how to use a PC, then I start learning how to code & today I'm proud to call myself a Web Developer which is not really true, I'm still learning HTML, CSS (SASS), JScritp, JQuery, Bootstrap, Node.Js, Markdown syntax, Jekill, GitHub & Git, etc. I can do simple websites (like this one) or blogs that my friends & neighbours can easily maintain a website without any knowledge of C.M.S., Databases, coding or even know how to host their own website & free of Ads.

I also repair all Windows or Linux systems, Desktop Computers, Laptops & Tablets free of charge to all my neighbours & friends. I never had a complaint & only once I wasn't able to reapair a Samsung ChromeBook due to the lack of support from Samsung in disclosing their Service manual, only for their technicians if you want to pay them the ransom (repair) money.

I also work w/ PhotoShop CS6 (W7 on VirtualBox), Darktable, UFRaw, Gimp, Atom Editor with Linux 18.2 Sonya XFCE, which is my favourite Linux system, and many others. I read a lot, I like to play the guitar & I'm good with kids at least that's what other people & neighbours say, coming from me will be pretentious ...
